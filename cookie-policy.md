This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how JiBrok ("we", "us", "our") uses them on [jibrok.com](https://jibrok.com), and how you can manage your preferences.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide analytics information, and remember your preferences.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Expiry |
|--------|----------|---------|--------|
| `cookie_consent_given` | jibrok.com | Records that you have made a cookie consent choice | 1 year |
| `cookie_consent_analytics` | jibrok.com | Stores your analytics cookie preference (granted/denied) | 1 year |
| `cookie_consent_functional` | jibrok.com | Stores your functional cookie preference (granted/denied) | 1 year |

### Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous usage data. They are only set if you click "Accept All" on the cookie banner.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Expiry |
|--------|----------|---------|--------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated ID | 2 years |
| `_ga_<container-id>` | Google Analytics | Maintains session state | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users (short-lived) | 24 hours |

We use Google Analytics 4 with the following privacy measures:
- **Google Consent Mode v2**: No analytics cookies are set until you provide explicit consent.
- **IP anonymization**: Your IP address is anonymized before processing.
- **No data sharing**: We do not share analytics data with Google for advertising purposes.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality such as our support widget. They are only set if you click "Accept All" on the cookie banner.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Expiry |
|--------|----------|---------|--------|
| Cookies set by JSD widget | Atlassian | Powers the Jira Service Desk support widget | Session |

## How to Manage Cookies

### On This Website

- **Cookie banner**: When you first visit, you can choose "Accept All", "Necessary Only", or "Customize" to select individual cookie categories.
- **Cookie Settings**: Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer at any time to change your preferences. You can enable or disable individual cookie categories using toggles, then click "Save Preferences".
- **Withdrawing consent**: Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer, disable the cookie categories you wish to opt out of, and click "Save Preferences". Any previously set analytics cookies will be deleted.

### In Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to instructions for common browsers:

- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the [Global Privacy Control](https://globalprivacycontrol.org/) browser signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal, analytics cookies will be automatically declined, as required by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).
